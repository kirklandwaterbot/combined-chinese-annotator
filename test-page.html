Context Test

Classical & Contextual Translation

「春眠不觉晓，处处闻啼鸟。」
「白日依山盡，黃河入海流。」

Contextual Resolution: OpenCC needs to determine correct mappings based on context. For example, 头发 (hair) vs 发现 (discover). In Traditional Chinese, "头发" is "頭髮", while "发现" is "發現".

Another example: 面粉 (flour) maps to 麵粉, but 表面 (surface) maps to 表面.
